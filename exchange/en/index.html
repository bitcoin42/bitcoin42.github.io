Summary

Creating a dezentralized exchange is as groundbreaking and paradigm shifting as the invention of Satashi Nakamotos Blockchain technology. It is the holy grail of Blockchain.

Centralized exchanges are not secure. Historically almost every major exchange has disappeared and stolen or lost billions of dollars of Bitcoins an escrow theft has been responsible for almost half of the Bitcoins in existence being stolen or lost.

The reason Bitcoin is successful is simply because it doesn’t require trust of third parties or banking institutions. It combines a shared history which makes it hard to shut down and easy to validate and difficult for a single group to control with hashing which can mathematically notarize anything and signatures which is used to prove identity. This combined with the open source code of Bitcoin allows anyone to read it and even start their own currencies. So it is required that a decentralized way to exchange any two coins must exist.

The design that does work is atomic trading which is currently the only way to connect any two blockchains. This is because it allows for asynchronous trustless trade based on a shared secret on both chains.
